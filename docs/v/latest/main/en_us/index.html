      Custom Great Patterns

      Merge Mind (entity, entity →)

      • Renders
        • Palettes
          Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: wqqwqwqaeqeeedqqeaqadedaqaedeqqeqedeqeaqeqaqedeadeaqwqwqaeda


          When the top villager iota of the stack is ready to level up, performs a mind-flay on the second-to-last entity iota of the stack and extract one random trade from it (if any).
          The extracted entity can also be a raider, wandering trader, etc.


          Infinity's Gambit

          • Renders
            • Palettes
              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: wewewewewewweeqeeqeeqeeqeeqee

              Resets the current spell execution counter. CAUTION: INFINITE LOOPS


              Area Crystallization

              • Renders
                • Palettes
                  Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: qwewewewewewdqeeeeedwwwawwqwwqwwwdedwwwqwwqwwwded

                  Destroys all creatures within the player's casting range and crystalize their vitality into media. The caster will suffer almost fatal backlash.


                  Flood-Fill Reflection (vector → list)

                  • Renders
                    • Palettes
                      Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: aaqawawaeadaadadadaadadadaada

                      BFS searches all blocks of the same type from the top vector iota of the stack, returns a list of vector iotas representing their positions.

                          Transplant's Exaltation (list, list, any → list)

                          • Renders
                            • Palettes
                              Your browser does not support visualizing patterns. Pattern code: wdwawedqdewawdw


                              List iota #0 is the nested list to be modified. According to the index sequence given by list #1, traverses step by step to the inner layer, and set the corresponding element to iota #2.
                              Returns the modified nested list.
